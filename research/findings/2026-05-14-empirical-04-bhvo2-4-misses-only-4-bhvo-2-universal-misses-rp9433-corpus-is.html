Empirical Finding: Only 4 BHVO-2 universal misses (rp=9433) — corpus is largely solved here

Source

Direct analysis of the n=33 CF-LIBS benchmark export, computed outside of Asta. Not generated by autodiscovery — manually verified.

Claim

BHVO-2 spectra (n=9, rp=9433) contribute only 4 of the 53 universal misses. This is the corpus where the existing identifier stack works well; further gains here are diminishing returns. The lower-rp regime appears to be a sweet spot for current line-matching + inversion.

What to do next

Audit the 4 misses individually — these are the most informative residuals because the surrounding 8 elements per spectrum were caught. Likely the misses are trace REE or very weak alkali lines. Confirm or rule out as a self-absorption case.
