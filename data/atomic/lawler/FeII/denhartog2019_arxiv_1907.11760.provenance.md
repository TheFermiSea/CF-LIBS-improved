## Why this PDF is staged (data it uniquely carries)
The AAS/IOP machine-readable supplement for this paper contains ONLY Table 6
(the 131-line log gf list, staged separately as denhartog2019_table6_mrt.txt).
Tables 3, 4 and 5 were verified to return HTTP `NoSuchKey` at IOP -> they are
NOT deposited as machine-readable files anywhere. They exist only inside this
PDF. The two that matter for lifetime-anchoring:

### Table 5 - "Radiative lifetimes used in this study" (tau)
- 25 upper levels, each with a radiative lifetime in nanoseconds and +/- error.
- Levels (term, J): z6Do 9/2,7/2,5/2,3/2,1/2 (5); z6Fo 11/2,9/2,7/2,5/2,3/2,1/2 (6);
  z6Po 7/2,5/2,3/2 (3); z4Fo 9/2,7/2,5/2,3/2 (4); z4Do 7/2,5/2,3/2,1/2 (4);
  z4Po 5/2,3/2,1/2 (3). Total = 25.
- Units: lifetime in ns (e.g. z6Do9/2 = 3.69 +/- 0.07 ns).
- IMPORTANT: these lifetimes are ADOPTED FROM THE LITERATURE, not newly measured
  in this paper. Reference keys in Table 5: a=Biemont et al. (1991),
  b=Schnabel et al. (2004), c=Hannaford et al. (1992), d=Guo et al. (1992).
- These 25 levels are exactly the 25 distinct upper levels of Table 6.

### Table 4 - "Branching fractions in Fe II" (BFs)
- Branching fractions for the 131 measured transitions, with comparison to
  Bergeson et al. (1996) and Schnabel et al. (2004). PDF-embedded only.
